Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

TravelM8 ("we," "our," or "us") is a road trip copilot app. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how we collect and use your data. This policy explains what information we gather, why we gather it, and how it is handled.

What We Collect

We collect only the information needed to provide the TravelM8 service:

  • Email address — collected when you create an account, used to identify your account and send service-related communications.
  • Trip data — routes, travel dates, traveler count, budget preferences, and stop selections you enter while planning trips.
  • Device information — a push notification token from your device, used solely to deliver departure reminders you opt into.

How We Use It

All data we collect is used exclusively to provide and improve the TravelM8 experience:

  • Saving your trips and preferences so they are available across sessions.
  • Planning and displaying routes and stop suggestions.
  • Sending departure reminders via push notification when you enable them.
  • Generating AI-powered route insights (see Third-Party Services below).

We do not sell your data to third parties, ever. We do not use your data for advertising or share it with marketing companies.

Data Storage

Your account and trip data are stored in a cloud database managed by Neon Postgres. Data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time (see User Rights below).

Data is stored in the United States. By using TravelM8 you consent to your data being processed there.

Third-Party Services

TravelM8 relies on the following third-party providers to deliver the service. Each provider has its own privacy policy governing their handling of data.

  • Vercel — hosts the TravelM8 web and API infrastructure. Standard server logs (IP address, request metadata) may be captured by Vercel per their privacy policy.
  • Neon Postgres — stores your account and trip data in a managed PostgreSQL database. See Neon's privacy policy.
  • Groq / Google Gemini — provides AI-generated route insights. When you request an AI insight, relevant trip data (route, dates, stop selections) is sent to the AI provider to generate a response. No personally identifiable information beyond trip context is included. See Groq's privacy policy and Google's privacy policy.
  • Expo — delivers push notifications to your device. Your device's push token is shared with Expo solely for this purpose. See Expo's privacy policy.

User Rights

You have control over your data:

  • Delete your account and data — email us at chintumatam@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account." We will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.
  • Cancel push notifications — you can disable departure reminders at any time from within the TravelM8 app settings. You can also revoke notification permission in your device's system settings.
  • Access or correct your data — email us at chintumatam@gmail.com and we will provide or correct your data upon request.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please reach out:
